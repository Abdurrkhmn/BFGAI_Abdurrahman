## 🎯 Learning Objectives

After completing this course, students will be able to:

- Understand the role of Generative AI in improving productivity and efficiency in both technical and non-technical fields  
- Use and develop Generative AI models to create ideas, content, and creative solutions faster  
- Improve career competitiveness with in-demand AI skills  
- Build a strong foundation to keep up with future Generative AI developments  

---

## 👥 Target Audience

This course is designed for:

- Anyone who wants to become a **Generative AI Engineer**  
- Students, professionals, or career switchers  
- People who are familiar with basic IT and can use a computer  

---

## 🧠 Prerequisites

Before taking this course, students should have:

- Basic understanding of **Artificial Intelligence**  
- Ability to design effective **prompts**  
- Knowledge of **Python programming**  
- Basic understanding of **Machine Learning** and **Deep Learning**  

---

## 📌 Requirements

To succeed in this course, students should:

- Be self-motivated and willing to learn independently  
- Be committed and consistent in practice  
- Have curiosity and strong interest in AI  

---

## 🏆 Expected Outcome

After finishing this course, students are expected to:

- Develop Generative AI projects  
- Use **image generation models** to create and edit images  
- Apply AI skills to real-world problems
